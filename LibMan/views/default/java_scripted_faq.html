How to book a reservation??

You firstly need to be registered on our database to reserve a book in our library.If you haven't registered yet please Register. Once you are done with that you can visit your profile page and book a reservation there.

How to cancel a reservation ?

Visit your profile page by logging in from the link given on the page.There you will find the link to cancel your reservation.

How to search for a book?

First log in to your profile and then search for the book.
